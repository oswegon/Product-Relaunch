Compose was created by a group of graffiti artists who were tired of faulty cans. We loved the work we created but wanted a can with good color and long lasting spray that's free of any faulty spray tips. At the same time, we wanted to keep artists and their technique in mind; to make sure our spray can be manipulated to create something unique on every canvas.

Three graffiti artists: Oswego, Brandon, and De'Andre shared the same vision for a better product that made any users project objective easier to reach.

Yes, we've gone through failers. 6 failed can designs have led us our successful can today.
